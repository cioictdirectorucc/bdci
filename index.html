We provide solutions to grow your business

The Business Development and Consulting Institute (UCC BD&CI) – is a key player in the implementation of Jamaica’s economic growth agenda. By investing time and resources in the delivery of business development services to large and emerging businesses, UCC BD&CI will help drive the startup and the growth of businesses. It is dedicated to help launching new entrepreneurs seeking to bring innovation to market, to enable firms in the sector to capitalize on business opportunities to launch new ventures and grow existing businesses.

The Business Research Unit is the next generation component of the Institute, which complements our consulting services. The research effort involves three elements: (1) research to support data and information for business advising, (2) research to support commercialization, and (3) policy-oriented research.
